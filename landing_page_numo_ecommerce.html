/// Why we publish specs

Every material, named. Every source, checked.

Most desk brands sell a mood. We sell a bill of materials. Each object lists its exact alloy, timber species, and finish — the same sheet we hand our own manufacturers.

If a supplier can't meet that sheet, we don't ship the object. That's cost us two launches this year. We're fine with that trade.
